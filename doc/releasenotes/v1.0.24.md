- Bump Microsoft.EntityFrameworkCore.Relational from 2.2.4 to 2.2.6
- Bump Microsoft.EntityFrameworkCore.SqlServer from 2.2.4 to 2.2.6
- Bump Microsoft.EntityFrameworkCore.Tools from 2.2.4 to 2.2.6
